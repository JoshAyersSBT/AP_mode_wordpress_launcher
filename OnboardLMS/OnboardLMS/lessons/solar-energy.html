Bonus Lesson: Introduction to Solar Energy

Solar energy is radiant light and heat from the Sun that is harnessed using a range of technologies, including solar photovoltaic (PV) panels and solar thermal collectors.

Key Concepts

  • Photovoltaic Effect: The process by which solar cells convert sunlight into electricity.
  • Solar Panels: Arrays of PV cells that generate power for homes and businesses.
  • Renewable Resource: Solar energy is sustainable and reduces reliance on fossil fuels.
